#+title: IFF v. Union of India (Section 69 IT Act)


1. The permissable purposes for which power is exercised under Section 69 are those with which 'legitimate public interest' is attached
2. The authorities that were empowered in this regard, had not been notified till 2018 (9 Years after the Act was passed)
